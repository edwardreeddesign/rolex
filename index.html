History

Rolex was founded by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London, 1905. Rolex base of operations is in Geneva, Switzerland in 1919. Rolex is the largest single luxury watch brand, producing about 2,000 watches per day, with estimated 2013 revenues of US$7.4 billion. Also, Rolex is the most reputable company that exclusively produces a consumer product. It employs over 2,800 employees.
